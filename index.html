Helping
professionals help people.

Acacia offers engaging live conferences and amazing downloadable content by leading global experts.

We are South Africa’s premier Continuing Professional Development (CPD) training provider for professionals in mental health, chemical health, and other associated disciplines. Our live seminars, in-house workshops, and online seminars and products are designed to elevate the practice of professionals operating in these disciplines.

Our mission is to connect psychiatrists, clinical, counseling, and educational psychologists, social workers, occupational therapists, counselors, and other health providers with the evidence-based knowledge and skills they need to grow in their practice and to help their patients and clients thrive.

CPD Approved Training

The Health Professional Council of South Africa has determined that webinars and products approved abroad for Continuing Medical Education purposes will be accepted in South Africa for CPD Points. Live seminars will be individually CPD accredited by South African boards.
