About the Acacia Team

Acacia Therapy and Health Training was founded by South African, Manuela Lopes, an occupational therapist, and American, Dr. Lane Pederson, a psychologist, author, and international trainer in Dialectical Behavioural Therapy (DBT).

Manuela and Lane originally teamed up to bring a DBT seminar to Johannesburg in August of 2016. The connection they made working together, along with their shared passion for professional growth, planted the seed that grew into Acacia Therapy and Health Training.

With our faculty of international experts and online trainings and products, Acacia provides the knowledge, methods, skills, and interventions professionals need to grow in their practice and serve their patients and clients.

Dr. Pederson has provided DBT training and consultation to over 10,000 professionals in the United States, Australia, South Africa, Dubai, Canada, and Mexico through his training and consultation company, Lane Pederson and Associates, LLC.
